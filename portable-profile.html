Projects

NBA return-to-play (internal load injuries)

5,000+ returns over 15 seasons. Some teams really do return players faster and keep them on the floor — and the gap doesn't disappear when you control for who they treat. ~18pp recurrence spread (p<0.001), ~17pp within-player.

Cumulative workload & player injuries

Capstone — Hebrew University. PSM + DiD, then ITT using schedule density, with a contact-injury falsification and a double-ITT carryover analysis. Dense exposure raises overuse risk by ~25% per game.
